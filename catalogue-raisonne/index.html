Catalogue Raisonné

The Burliuk Foundation is currently in the process of preparing the Catalogue Raisonné of the work of David Davidovich Burliuk. The Burliuk Foundation welcomes anyone that owns a picture by David Davidovich Burliuk to register their work with the Foundation.
